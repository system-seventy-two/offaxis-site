"OffAxis MTB Gyro System"

Created for my High School Senior Keystone project as a means of learning CAD and CNC machining. A gyro is a component that lets the bars on a bike spin infinitely while retaining the ability to have mechanical brakes. My design process included digital prototyping with Fusion360 (CAD) and Blender, in addition to physical prototyping with both resin and FDM 3D printers. Final product was milled from 6061 T6 aluminum.

To increase accessibility to these parts, I’ve made all design files and installation instructables available for [download].
