"SR1, Railblazer"

Railblazer, a steel single rail roller coaster prototype developed by Rocky Mountain Construction, opened at California's Great America in June 2018. A sister model featuring a mirrored layout, opened the same year at Six Flags Fiesta Texas.

The ride's single seat eight car train design are both one of the most visually striking and technically impressive elements of the train. They featured CNC'd wheel bogies, ratcheting hybrid lap/OTS restraints, and a unique air pressurization system which controls restraint locking.

Modeled in Blender and printed on Elegoo Mars SLA 3D printer
