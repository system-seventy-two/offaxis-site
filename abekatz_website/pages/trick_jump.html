"Oakland Pumptrack MTB Trick Jump"

This jump, as seen in [Safety Third] was constructed as part of a two-week expansion project at the Oakland Pumptrack proposed and executed by myself and two others. During the course of this project, a mulch landing (for soft landings), a roller, and a 6.5ft tall wooden jump with a 12ft radius were built. I designed the takeoff in Illustrator and created the model to the right in Blender based on the scaled SVG files.

This jump is now open to the public at the Oakland Pumptrack.
