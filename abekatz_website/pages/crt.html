"Everybody, and Nobody"

3D simulation of a live multi-channel video instillation using CRT televisions, presented within an animated Mac OS 9 environment. Each screen features a looping cycle of 394 (the number of admitted college freshman at my school the year prior to my enrollment) artificially generated faces, symbolizing the feeling of being surrounded by new people at college. 3D work done in Blender, 2D work done in After Effects. Created for an assignment in Reed College's Art 196F: Digital Video and Coding Interactivity.
